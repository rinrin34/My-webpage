📝 My Poem

If I will be asked what is living for me. I'll say it is doing or becoming what you really wanted. It is finding your own personal legend and never give up on it. Because when you want something with all your heart, "all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it."

This poem is based on the book i recently read, and i realized a lot of things by reading it. We all have our Personal Legend, you just have to not ever give up on it, everything is just part of our journey you don't have to rush things, instead slow down and look how beautiful it is.

A shepherd leaves the hills he knows behind,
To chase a dream the desert sands concealed.
Through wind and stone,
he learns to seek and find
The language that the silent heavens yield.
For gold is but a shadow of the soul,
A marker on the path where omens lead,
As every heart discovers its true role
By planting faith within a dreamer’s seed.


The world conspires to help the brave of heart,
Who dare to cross the dunes of doubt and fear.
To find the whole, one must embrace the part,
And listen till the inner voice is clear.
No treasure lies as deep as what we gain
When we transform like lead into the sun,
For joy is forged through every test and pain
Until the dream and dreamer live as one.
